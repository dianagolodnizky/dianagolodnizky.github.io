CASE STUDY

SUCRÉE
CHIC

A premium patisserie website featuring an integrated menu hub, a streamlined e-commerce shop, and a custom gift box design platform.

View on Figma

ABOUT

Sucrée Chic is a premium Parisian-inspired patisserie specializing in artisanal pastries, bespoke gift boxes, and high-end catering. The brand approached us to design and develop a comprehensive digital platform that would elevate their online presence and enable customers to browse the menu, shop for gifts, and create fully personalized gift box experiences — all in one cohesive environment.

The project required a deep understanding of luxury branding paired with complex e-commerce functionality — achieving a precise balance between visual elegance and streamlined, intuitive user flows that serve a diverse audience of individual shoppers and corporate clients.

PROBLEM
STATEMENT

Sucrée Chic lacked a cohesive digital presence. Without an integrated online platform, they struggled to communicate their premium brand identity, showcase their full product range, and provide customers with a convenient way to explore, order, and personalize their offerings — resulting in missed revenue opportunities and a fragmented, inconsistent brand experience across every customer touchpoint.

SOLUTION

We designed and developed a fully integrated premium website combining three core experiences: a beautifully curated menu hub, a seamless e-commerce shop, and a unique custom gift box design platform. Every touchpoint was crafted to reflect the brand's luxury identity while ensuring an intuitive and frictionless user journey — from first discovery to final checkout.

The result is a cohesive, high-performance platform that reinforces brand trust, drives conversions, and positions Sucrée Chic as the premier choice for discerning patisserie lovers and corporate gifting clients alike.

OBJECTIVES
& GOALS

CONVENIENCE & CONTROL

Enable customers to independently browse, customize, and order products without needing direct contact or assistance at any stage.

CREATIVE FREEDOM

Allow users to design fully personalized gift boxes with complete autonomy over contents, packaging, and presentation styles.

CLARITY & TRUST

Build purchasing confidence through transparent product details, pricing, ingredients, and allergen information across every product.

ALL IN ONE EXPERIENCE

Unify the menu, shop, and box customizer into a single seamless platform accessible from any device, at any time.

SEAMLESS E-COMMERCE

Enable smooth, friction-free transactions with a fully integrated checkout flow and intuitive order management.

BUSINESS
CHALLENGES

CLEARING THE BRAND EXPERIENCE

Translating the elevated in-store sensory experience — the aromas, textures, and craftsmanship — into a compelling and authentic digital format that preserves the brand's luxury essence without feeling sterile.

NUTRITIONAL SOURCING

Integrating accurate, dynamic ingredient and allergen information across the full product catalog while keeping the interface clean, visual, and uncluttered for users who simply want to browse.

DESIGNING A FULLY CUSTOM BOX

Creating an intuitive gift box builder that makes complex, multi-step customization feel effortless and visually satisfying — accessible to first-time users without sacrificing depth for experienced ones.

CUSTOMER RETENTION

Designing engagement touchpoints and loyalty mechanisms that encourage repeat purchases and long-term brand affinity far beyond the initial transaction.

TARGET
AUDIENCE

THE THOUGHTFUL GIFTER

THE MILESTONE CELEBRATION

Couples and families marking life events — weddings, baby showers, graduations — with premium confections that serve as edible centerpieces and deeply memorable keepsakes for every guest.

THE AESTHETIC SOCIAL SHOPPER

Trend-aware consumers drawn to the brand's visual identity, packaging aesthetics, and social media presence. Discovery-driven shoppers who convert through compelling visual storytelling and shareable, beautifully crafted product experiences that photograph as well as they taste.
